Pork Tenderloin with Roasted Cherries

Some friendly markdown

Timing Preparation Time 15m Cook Time 35m Ingredients 2 tablespoons olive oil divided 1.5 teaspoons kosher salt divided 0.25 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper 1 teaspoon ground cumin 0.25 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1.25 pounds pork tenderloin excess fat and silver skin removed Directions 1 Preheat oven to 425 degrees. 2 Heat a large ovenproof nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add 1 tablespoon oil; swirl to coat. 3 Combine 1 teaspoon salt, pepper, cumin, cinnamon and ginger. Rub pork evenly with this spice mixture. 4 Add pork to now-hot skillet; saute 4 minutes. 5 Turn pork over; place pan in oven and roast for 15 minutes, or until an instant-read thermometer registers 140 degrees. 6 Remove pork from pan; place on a cutting board or plate and tent loosely with foil. Do not wipe out pan. 7 While pork is resting, add remaining 1 tablespoon oil to pan; swirl to coat.
